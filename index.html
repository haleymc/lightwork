The Work

My favorite collaborators are talented, ambitious leaders in foggy conditions who could use a mirror, divining rod, or compass tune-up.

How we work

Consigliere

Ongoing mode. Residency and consigliere engagements — seasonal or retained. Regular sessions, light async access, and a consistent rhythm. This is where most relationships begin.

Incubator

Project mode. Co-building for launches, new chapters, and builds that need a strategic co-author embedded in the work. Limited slots per season.
